About

A mission beyond the boundaries of conventional science.

Silent Star Service operates at the intersection of quantum physics, deep space research, and advanced signal processing. Our work addresses questions that traditional institutions are not yet equipped to ask.

We do not publish our methodologies. We do not confirm our partnerships. We do not discuss our timeline. What we can confirm — something has been trying to reach us for a very long time.

Research Divisions

Where the signal ends,
our work begins.

Three active research divisions. All classified at varying levels. Summaries available upon verified clearance request.

Quantum Signal Processing
Analysis of a gravitational anomaly first detected at the Cretaceous-Paleogene boundary — 65 million years ago. Three synchronized emission sources identified — Earth, Moon, Jupiter — forming a triangle whose central axis points toward a location 150 AU from our sun.

This is not a natural phenomenon. This is a navigation system. Someone left markers. The question is — for whom.
"This is not a trace of another civilization. This is a navigation system." — Director N. Jensen, 2021
Division 01 — Active
Tachyonic Communication
The Division began with a theoretical problem — faster-than-light data transmission. The physics reached its limit. Then a terminal was assembled overnight in the central wing. Nobody saw it completed.

Four weeks of analysis later — Dr. Johnson's conclusion — the terminal does not transmit toward the future. It receives from it. Current data suggests transmissions originate approximately 32 years ahead of our present moment.
"The machine receives something. We are still learning to decode it." — Dr. W. Johnson, 2023
Division 02 — Classified
Xenomorphic Material Analysis
Study of sealed components delivered to our Harrison County facility. The material defies every known physical model. Perfect insulator and perfect conductor simultaneously. Impervious to heat, cold, radiation, and pressure. No X-ray or gamma scanner can reveal its interior structure.

The material appears to be aware of observation. It responds. The Division's current working hypothesis — this is not a manufactured material. It is an organ.
"She built this for something that hasn't happened yet." — Dr. W. Johnson, 2024
